---
title: Export Data
description:
  Learn how Anchor stores and shares your data securely.
---

Anchor securely stores your data and logs on decentralized
storage systems like **Storacha (IPFS)**. Whenever a goal is
achieved using Anchor, the associated logs and conversations
are securely saved on the **Filecoin** network, ensuring
only you have access.

Additionally, if you enable Telegram logs, these updates
will be sent to your Telegram account as well. To activate
Telegram logging, simply click the **Telegram icon** in the
chat menu.
